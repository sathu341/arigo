    About Us

    Kothamangalam Agriculture Producer Company Ltd. (KAPCO) is a farmer-owned collective established in 2016 with the support of NABARD. Created to bring local farmers together under one platform. KAPCO helps them process, add value and market their products in an organized and transparent way. Through our collection centres, processing units, and retail networks, we handle a wide range of products including coconut oil, spices, food-grain powders, natural products, fruits, vegetables, rubber, and more. Built on trust, cooperation and community strength, KAPCO represents a genuine effort to support farmers and deliver authentic, locally sourced products to consumers.

    Services

    Our Service Towards Farmers And Customer

    Collection & Marketing

    Collecting and selling local fruits, vegetables & natural products through trusted outlets.

    Coconut Processing

    Producing copra and manufacturing pure coconut oil under the KAPCO brand.

    Dry Fruits & Vegetable Processing

    Converting locally grown vegetables like bitter gourd, banana, and ivy gourd into dehydrated products.

    Spice & Grain Powders Curry Powder

    Producing curry powders and staple powders like appam podi, puttu podi, wheat & ragi flour.

    Agricultural Input Supply

    Providing organic/chemical fertilizers and rubber-farmer essentials.

    Natural Products Marketing

    Ghee, honey, egg, vinegar, and other local farmer products marketed under KAPCO.

    One Open Market

    One open market at Kothamangalam for the marketing of Agri products collected from farmers.

    Equipment Rental

    Renting essential farm tools like brush cutters, sprayers and more

    Rubber Processing

    Collecting latex and producing Rubber sheets and ammoniated latex.

    Agro Tourism

    Promoting farm-based tourism activities with NABARD support, including scheduled tours using company-owned vehicles.
